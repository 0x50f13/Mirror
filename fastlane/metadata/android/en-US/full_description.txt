ABOUT
This application allows you to use your device as a mirror by streaming your frontal camera. It is open-source, free to use and does not require any extra permissions except camera.

FEATURES
* Pause stream, so you can better look on some aspects of yourself
* Material 3 design
* Convenient GUI
* Keeps screen on
* Full-screen image
